Welcome!

Below are the surviving tabs from my old website, walkerguitar.com. Some were lost in a server crash, but I ended up recovering a fair amount of them. I also added plenty of tabs from my personal teaching. All these tabs are free of charge based on the take-a-penny, leave-a-penny donation model, so please consider sending me whatever you feel is fair--much appreciated! All tabs for YouTube lessons have the lesson links next to them so you can study from here as needed. Feel free to bookmark this page for future visits.

I am doing private lessons on Zoom these days, so if you need some personal help please reach out here!

Happy playing!
