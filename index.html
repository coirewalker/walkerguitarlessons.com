About Me

I have been playing guitar for twenty years. I have been teaching people how to play guitar for eleven years. I earned my music degree in 2005 from Musicians Institute in Los Angeles. I like to balance the technical with the musical in order to build people into healthy, creative, thinking musicians over the longterm.

You can see me teach on my YouTube channel here.

Mostly I just enjoy sharing how cool I think music is, while helping my students get where they want to be. Oh, and I live and teach from my home in Prospect Heights, Brooklyn. I like it there :)
