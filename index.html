About Me

Hi! My name is Coire Walker and I am a musician and guitar teacher. I recently relocated from Brooklyn, NY, to my hometown of San Diego. I have been playing guitar for twenty-two years, and I've been teaching people how to play guitar for thirteen of those years. My music degree is from Musicians Institute in Los Angeles, CA. In my approach to teaching I like to balance the technical with the musical in order to build people into healthy, creative, thinking musicians over the longterm.

My favorite thing about teaching is sharing how cool music is, while helping my students get where they want to be--the longterm process and working relationships are what it's all about. All ages are welcome, from children, to adults, to older adults (it's never too late!) The youngest student I ever taught was 4 years old--the oldest was 76.

Regarding musical styles, I teach all genres of music except advanced jazz and classical guitar--these are their own discipline and I do not claim to have mastery of them (but we can play with them as much as you like!) Some styles I enjoy are folk, rock, metal, punk, reggae, funk, RNB, indie, post rock, and hip hop. Some of the lesson topics I teach include music theory, reading, ear training, strumming and picking techniques, learning to sing and play simultaneously, songwriting approaches, soloing, chord composition and harmony, learning to play songs you love, and even working on YOUR own original material! If you are in a band and looking to challenge your playing further I can work alongside you in that regard as well.

Most importantly, my goal as a teacher is to help you enjoy the musical process as we move forward together.

I am available to provide live music for events like weddings, art galleries, social gatherings, and especially all-expenses-paid pleasure cruises (winter season preferred, but negotiable). I have good working relationships with many local musicians and could assemble a custom band to fit your needs, if that is something you require.
