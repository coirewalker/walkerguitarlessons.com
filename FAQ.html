Lesson Policy

Please understand that although I am deeply passionate about teaching guitar, it is still my career and primary income. Therefore, I have to abide by strict policies in order to keep my business sustainable. Please reach out with any questions or concerns. Thanks for your understanding.

  • I require all payments be made at the time we schedule a lesson to reserve our lesson slot. If we agree on a regular weekly lesson time, I require payments for the month in advance.
  • Payment methods are Venmo, PayPal (the no fee option), ApplePay, and cash.
  • Lesson cancellations and reschedules require 24hrs notice. Any cancellations after 24hrs are considered paid in-full. I do my best to remain flexible for rescheduling, but I cannot guarantee it.
  • Students receive text confirmation 48hrs prior to our lesson time as a courtesy reminder; this allows 24hrs to cancel or reschedule before forfeiting lesson payment.
  • I will send a reoccuring Zoom session link for online students; please bookmark it for future use.
